Featured Case Study

Alpine Legacy — eXp Realty CO

A 3-year embedded SMM engagement that rebuilt a real estate team's entire digital identity across six platforms — and let it compound into back-to-back ICON awards.

Dec 2022 — Nov 2025

From a near-invisible digital footprint to 2× ICON.

Alpine Legacy had inconsistent presence across every platform — 512-follower LinkedIn, underperforming Facebook video, untouched Instagram, and no GBP strategy. The brief: rebuild the full digital identity from scratch and let it compound without paid ads or posting chaos.

2022
Full baseline audit — 6 platforms
SWOT across LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, GBP. Identified gaps: no brand coherence, low video strategy, 512 LinkedIn connections, 225 IG followers, no YouTube channel setup.
2023
Full rebuild — content systems deployed
52-week content calendar authored. LinkedIn daily connection cadence (20/day) launched. Reels strategy deployed. Property landing pages built in KVCore + Luxury Presence with MailChimp email sequences. GBP activated.
2024
Compounding — ICON Year 2
FB video watch-time up 181.3% YoY. Reels reaching 95.1% non-follower audiences. 15% YoY community engagement growth, 25% lead generation increase. Second consecutive ICON secured.
2025
Authority compounded — 7,700+ LinkedIn
LinkedIn audience reaches 7,700+ from 512 baseline. 86 total transactions influenced over the engagement. Zero paid ad spend across all 3 years.
